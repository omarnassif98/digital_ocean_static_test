purgatorium of things [2022 - ongoing]

What if "things" don't die. What if the whole idea that the observer determines the existence also applies to the inanimate.

Through these images, the photographer proposes a "purgatorium of things". A place where items of the vernacular linger in latency until once again the photographes are seen by the observer.
