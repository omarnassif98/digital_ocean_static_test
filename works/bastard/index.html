bastard series [2016 - 2022]

For a brief time, experiments were made using digital photographyin an effort adequate the photographer to his anthropocene. Needless to say, this period did not last more than 6 months and the photographs were never given parenthood; never accepted as his.

Through the artifices of what today is called "alternative photography", but should actually be called "traditional photography", the photographer finds ease in accepting these images as his own and giving them his last name. Thus, this series is called "bastard series".
