wormhole

There is a certain lure to the non-place - places not meant for relationships - as presented by Marc Augé. Non-places have, in essence, the purpose to reduce one’s individuality to nullity. You find that in the hipermarkets, big chain hotels where the business man is guest for not more than a night, the never-ending airport terminals, the subway systems in big cities, etc.

This project aims at giving the non-place a second glimpse - an attempt to build a relationship with the physical space of the non-place using the camera as a moderator.

Started in September 2019 focusing on the Parisian Metropolitain system, this project is still far from being completed.
